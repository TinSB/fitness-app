Lists & widgets → glyph

The high-frequency scanning surfaces stay glyph + label: faster recognition at small sizes, zero emotional/appetite cue, and 100% long-tail coverage with no photo library to maintain. Widgets never use photos (30 MB memory budget).

Detail hero → photo

Photos earn their place only where the user has actively tapped in. Flat light, no gloss/steam/sauce. Just the ~40–60 most-used produce/dairy/grain items get a shot — small, consistent, controllable. Dark mode uses a semantic warm-white card, never a raw white block.

Raw meat → glyph + a glyph-mode switch

Raw meat is always a glyph (clinical, disgust-prone, most off-tone for ED safety). A global "glyph mode" toggle flips every photo back to glyphs for sensitive users — a zero-imagery safe state.

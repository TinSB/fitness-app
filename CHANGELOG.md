- **M1-3 `RedeDataHealth` clean view (minimal) — M1 data-foundation milestone complete**: rebuilt `ios/packages/RedeDataHealth` as the read-only purification layer between raw AppData and everything downstream (TDD red→green, 20 tests). `CleanAppDataViewBuilder.build(from:)` is a pure value transformation: raw AppData is carried verbatim and never rewritten; dirty/missing data is dropped (never silently patched) with every drop honestly recorded as a typed `DataHealthIssue` — these issues are also the future data-trust signal for the Progress page (MVP success criterion 5).
